# TWIN-EB — Landing-page tile images · AI prompt brief

Seven background images for the "Explore the project" tiles on the TWIN-EB landing page.
This brief can be handed to a designer or pasted directly into an AI image tool (Midjourney,
DALL·E, Adobe Firefly, Ideogram, etc.). Each tile prompt below is **self-contained and paste-ready**.

The seven images should read as **one cohesive set** — same muted palette, same soft abstract style.

## How to use

- **Format:** landscape, aspect ratio ≈ **16:10** (e.g. **1600 × 1000 px**), exported as **JPG**.
- **Filenames & location:** save with these exact names in `docs/assets/img/` — the tiles pick them up automatically:

  | Tile | File |
  |---|---|
  | 01 · EB & RDEB | `tile-eb-rdeb.jpg` |
  | 02 · Losartan in RDEB | `tile-losartan.jpg` |
  | 03 · Virtual Twins | `tile-virtual-twins.jpg` |
  | 04 · The project | `tile-project.jpg` |
  | 05 · Team | `tile-team.jpg` |
  | 06 · Publications | `tile-publications.jpg` |
  | 07 · For families | `tile-families.jpg` |

- **Important composition rule:** the **lower third** of each tile is automatically covered by a dark teal
  gradient with small white caption text. Keep that area **calm and uncluttered**, and put the visual
  interest in the **upper two thirds**.

## Palette reference (match across all tiles)

Muted **petrol teal `#135c6c`**, **deep blue-green `#0c3a44`**, **mid teal `#1a7186`**, warm **sand / off-white
`#faf7f2` / `#f3ede3`**, and a warm **coral accent `#e06a4b`** used *very sparingly* (a single highlight).
Low saturation, soft diffuse light, gentle depth, subtle film grain. Calm, scientific, hopeful.

## Global negative prompt (applies to every image)

> No text, letters, words, numbers, captions, logos, watermarks, signatures or UI elements; no identifiable
> human faces; no gore, wounds, blood, blisters, rashes, needles or medical distress; no clinical sterility
> or coldness; no cluttered or chaotic composition; avoid stock-photo clichés (no stethoscopes, no generic
> handshakes, no lab-coat portraits).

## 01 · EB & RDEB — `tile-eb-rdeb.jpg`

**Concept:** Softly overlapping, translucent membranes with a faint butterfly-wing venation, catching gentle light — an abstract, dignified metaphor for the extreme delicacy of RDEB skin ("butterfly children"), never depicting injury.

**Prompt:**
```
A soft, modern abstract editorial illustration, conceptual and 3D-ish: several sheer, translucent overlapping membranes drifting and layering like the delicate skin of a butterfly's wing, edges catching soft diffuse light, with the faintest suggestion of fine wing-like veining and gentle iridescence. Composition places all visual interest in the upper two thirds — luminous layered membranes floating amid generous negative space — while the lower third stays calm, quiet and near-empty so a dark teal gradient and small white caption sit cleanly over it. Palette of muted petrol teal, deep blue-green and mid teal with warm sand and off-white, plus a single restrained warm-coral highlight glinting on one membrane edge. Low saturation, soft depth, subtle film grain, hopeful and tender mood. Landscape, roughly 16:10. No text, letters, numbers, logos, watermarks or UI; no human faces; no wounds, blisters, rashes, blood or medical distress; no clinical coldness; no clutter; no stock-photo cliches.
```

**Variation:** Extreme close abstraction of two or three overlapping gossamer skin-like layers, edges translucent and catching a soft petrol-teal glow with one faint coral rim-light, vast off-white negative space above and a hushed, calm lower third for the caption overlay.

**Also avoid:** literal butterflies or whole butterfly silhouettes; realistic epidermis or macro skin photography; oversaturated rainbow iridescence; busy or cluttered wing patterns; heavy dark elements in the upper two thirds.

Muted petrol teal (#135c6c), deep blue-green (#0c3a44) and mid teal (#1a7186) over a warm sand off-white ground (#faf7f2), with a single small warm coral (#e06a4b) sphere as one quiet highlight. Negative: no text, letters, numbers, logos, watermarks or UI; no human faces; no gore, wounds, blood, needles or medical distress; no clinical coldness; no clutter; no pharmacy shelves, no pills or blister packs, no stethoscopes.

**Variation:** A single softly glowing translucent capsule suspended in the upper third, emitting faint teal filaments that fan out and settle taut fibres into relaxed curves below; warm sand ground, one coral glint of light, calm darker lower third left open for captions.

**Also avoid:** pharmacy shelves; pill bottles or blister packs; chemical-formula text or labels; stiff ball-and-stick clip-art; oversaturated neon chemistry; busy molecules crowding the lower third.

**Also avoid:** busy circuit-board detail; literal flowchart boxes and arrows; hard technical CAD linework; neon / high-saturation glow; tangled or chaotic web of lines; cold sterile tech aesthetic.
