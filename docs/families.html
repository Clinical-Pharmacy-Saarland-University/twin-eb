This page explains the LESS-EB study in plain terms and how to register your interest.

About the study

LESS-EB is a clinical study for children and young people with recessive dystrophic epidermolysis bullosa. It measures how the body handles losartan and how the disease responds, so that dosing can be based on evidence rather than estimate. The study is led by Prof. Dr. Dimitra Kiritsi and takes place at specialist EB centres in Germany and Greece.

What taking part would involve

A few blood samples

Typically 2–4, to measure losartan and its metabolite, planned to fit around usual care.

Routine assessments

Biomarkers of inflammation, disease activity and growth, with standard safety checks.

Specialist centres

Visits take place at established EB centres in Germany and Greece.

Registering your interest

You can register your interest at any time, and we will keep you informed as the study opens. Registering is not a commitment and does not enrol a child in the study. Whether a child is eligible is decided together with the clinical team, in line with the approved protocol, and recruitment begins only once approvals are in place.
