Investigators and collaborators

Prof. Dr. Thorsten Lehr
Lead applicant · Pharmacometrics
Clinical Pharmacy, Saarland University, Germany. Leads the PBPK, virtual twin, population PK/PD and decision-support work.

Professor of Clinical Pharmacy and a trained pharmacist specialising in pharmacometrics and model-informed drug development, previously principal pharmacometrician at Boehringer Ingelheim.

Prof. Dr. Dimitra Kiritsi
Co-applicant · Clinical lead
Dermatology, Medical Center – University of Freiburg and Aristotle University of Thessaloniki. Leads the LESS-EB clinical study.

Consultant dermatologist and professor specialising in epidermolysis bullosa, with extensive experience in EB clinical trials, including as principal investigator of the REFLECT study.

Prof. Dr. Matthias Schwab
Collaborator · Bioanalytics
Dr. Margarete Fischer-Bosch Institute of Clinical Pharmacology, Stuttgart, and University of Tübingen. Bioanalysis and metabolomics.

Paediatrician and clinical pharmacologist and director of the Dr. Margarete Fischer-Bosch Institute of Clinical Pharmacology in Stuttgart, with expertise in pharmacogenomics and personalised medicine.

Funders and partners

TWIN-EB is a joint research grant funded through the DEBRA International and LifeArc repurposing programme, carried out with academic and clinical partners and the EB community.

DEBRA International Medical Center – University of Freiburg Fischer-Bosch Institute (IKP), Stuttgart

The project also works with the wider EB community, including DEBRA organisations and the EB-ClinNet and EB-ResNet clinical networks.
