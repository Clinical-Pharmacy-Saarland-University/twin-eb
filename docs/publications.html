Advancing rare disease therapeutics through digital twins: Opportunities in drug development and precision dosing

Dette CMU, Alberg V, Rüdesheim S, Selzer D, Marok FZ, Bragazzi NL, Fuhr LM, Brunak S, Pearson ER, Zahn T, Kiritsi D, Schwab M, Lehr T

Computational and Structural Biotechnology Journal 2025;28:592–608

This review examines how digital twins are used in rare-disease research: the modelling techniques involved (physiologically based and population pharmacokinetic modelling, quantitative systems pharmacology and physiome modelling), why current models do not yet qualify as true digital twins, and their potential for drug development, disease-progression modelling and clinical decision support. Recessive dystrophic epidermolysis bullosa is used as a worked example, and the paper sets out the modular twin architecture that TWIN-EB implements.
